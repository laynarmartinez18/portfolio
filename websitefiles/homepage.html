// About Me

I think about who's on the other end of everything.

My whole career has been about one question: what actually gets through? Not what looks good in a deck — what connects, moves people, changes behavior, and sticks.

I've built digital brands from the ground up, led data-driven communications for a major transit authority, optimized e-commerce experiences to the tune of $500K in new revenue, and helped a world-class research organization completely rethink how it delivers information to its users.

Whether I'm mapping a 8,000-person workforce into a Tableau database or rewriting a homepage at midnight before a launch, my obsession is the same: make it land.

Boston-based. Open to remote and hybrid. Always looking for the next hard problem.
